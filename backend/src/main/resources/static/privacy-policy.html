Fantasy Watch Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-08

Fantasy Watch is a rewards application where users can watch rewarded ads, earn credits, invite friends, and request cashouts. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it.

Information We Collect

  • Email address and authentication identifiers from Firebase Authentication
  • Country/region selected during signup
  • Wallet balances, ledger history, referral activity, and cashout requests
  • Device and security signals used for fraud prevention
  • Technical diagnostics and crash reports

How We Use Information

  • To create and manage user accounts
  • To grant credits after validated rewarded ad completion
  • To review and process cashout requests
  • To prevent fraud, abuse, fake referrals, and duplicate accounts
  • To improve app reliability, analytics, and support

Third-Party Services

Fantasy Watch uses trusted third-party providers including Firebase Authentication, Railway-hosted backend infrastructure, PayPal for payouts, and AppLovin MAX / mediated ad networks for rewarded ads. These providers may process limited data required to deliver their services.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We only share data with service providers needed to operate authentication, fraud prevention, analytics, ads, and payouts.

Security

We use Firebase token verification, backend validation for rewards, server-side fraud checks, and administrative review for payouts. No system can guarantee absolute security, but we apply reasonable technical safeguards.

Children

Fantasy Watch is not intended for children under 18 and should only be used by people legally allowed to receive rewards and payouts in their country.

Your Rights

You may request account review or deletion by contacting support. Some financial and anti-fraud records may be retained where required for compliance and audit purposes.
