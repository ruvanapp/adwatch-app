## Full Description

Fantasy Watch is a rewards app where users can watch rewarded ads, earn credits, invite friends, and request cashouts.

Features:

- Secure sign up with email or Google
- Watch rewarded video ads and earn credits
- Wallet with earnings and transaction history
- Invite friends and earn referral rewards
- Manual cashout review for safer payouts
- Arabic and English language support

Fantasy Watch uses backend validation, fraud prevention checks, and admin review to help keep rewards fair and secure.

Users should only use the app if rewards and payout services are legal in their country.
